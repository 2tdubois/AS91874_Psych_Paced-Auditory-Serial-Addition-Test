Instructions

You will be listening to 1-digit numbers. Under a 3-second time limit, you will be entering the sum of the number you hear and the number you heard before it. For example, if you heard the numbers 9, 7, 3, and 5, you would enter 16 after hearing 7, you would enter 10 after hearing 3, and you would enter 8 after hearing 5. You will input these numbers on the webpage, along with pressing a button to submit your answer. If you do not answer in time, the test will skip to the next number. Confirm first with the experimenter that you understand the task.
